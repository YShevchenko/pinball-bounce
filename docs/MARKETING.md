# Pinball Bounce Marketing & UA Strategy

## Viability Classification
**Hyper-Casual / Arcade Market**
High volume, low margins. Requires relentless creative testing.

## Target Audience & Ad Platforms
- **Primary:** TikTok Ads and Meta (Facebook/Instagram).
- **Format:** 10-second gameplay videos showing a "fail" to trigger the user to download it and prove they can do it better.

## Core Hook
"Procedural neon pinball."

## Geographic Strategy
- **Tier 1 (US/UK/AUS):** High eCPMs. Acquire users here to drive maximum ad revenue.
- **Tier 3 (Brazil/India):** Low eCPMs but very cheap CPIs. Use to drive raw download numbers to boost store rankings.
